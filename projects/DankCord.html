About

DankCord is the first Python library designed and made specifically for automating the Discord bot, Dank Memer.
Our vision is for it to help people create their very own autofarms, using DankCord,as there is almost
no autofarms at this moment besides WhiteFlow that will be made soon.
DankCord is still under heavy construction, so stay tuned for the upcoming updates!
