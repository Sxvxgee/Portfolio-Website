Project YouTubeTools

Tools for YouTube, in the form of a command line application, as well as a Discord & Guilded bot.

About

YouTubeTools is an ambitious project that manifests itself in three distinct manifestations: a Discord bot, a Guilded bot, and a Command Line script. The Discord bot aspect distinguishes itself by employing a conventional Discord Bot instead of a selfbot, as the comprehensive permissions and functionalities associated with selfbots are deemed unnecessary. While the Guilded bot may experience occasional updates due to limited usage, it remains a viable option for certain individuals, although the majority of users often gravitate towards the Command Line script or the Discord bot version. Notably, the Command Line version emerges as the epitome of sophistication among the three, owing to its streamlined development process, devoid of integrations with external platforms, solely relying on the terminal itself and the script's native environment. This multi-faceted endeavor is driven by a paramount objective: to democratize the accessibility of indispensable utilities by presenting them in diverse formats, thereby ensuring a wider audience can avail themselves of their benefits.
